Location announcements

  • Due to higher-than-anticipated registration numbers, Thursday's sessions have been moved to the Student Activity Center Ballroom, which is located on the second floor of the SAC (2.410). We will be on the far (north) half of the ballroom.
  • The reception following the Thursday sessions will still be held in the College of Liberal Arts building, on the ground floor, in the Glickman Conference center (CLA 1.302).
  • Friday, Saturday, and Sunday sessions will take place in the Edgar A. Smith building (2nd floor).

About

The 26th conference on Semantics and Linguistic Theory (SALT) will be held at the University of Texas at Austin from Thursday, May 12, to Sunday, May 15, 2016.

The conference will begin with a special session on presupposition on Thursday (May 12), followed by the main session, which will run Friday to Sunday (May 13–15).

The Proceedings of SALT 26 will be published by Cornell and the LSA, as usual, and will be available at the SALT Proceedings webpage once published. If you want to join the LSA, they are offering SALT participants a 20% discount on membership fees.

Invited Speakers

  • Rick Nouwen, Utrecht University
  • Kristen Syrett, Rutgers University
