Announcements!

The 26th conference on Semantics and Linguistic Theory (SALT) will be held at the University of Texas at Austin from Thursday, May 12, to Sunday, May 15, 2016. (Click here to add this to your Google Calendar.)

The conference will begin with a special session on presupposition on Thursday (May 12), followed by the main session, which will run Friday to Sunday (May 13–15).

You can find our Linguist List announcement, which conveys a subset of the information on this site, here.

Invited Speakers

  • Sigrid Beck, University of Tübingen
  • Edit Doron, The Hebrew University of Jerusalem
  • Rick Nouwen, Utrecht University
  • Kristen Syrett, Rutgers University
